Bistré - how it all started

It all started with a passion for fresh food. Bistré has been the restaurant of choice for many locals in Hämeenlinna since 1986. We started with 20 seats, and extended two years later with an addition of 22 seats. Now, we are serving more than 50 customers daily.

All of our food is made fresh in house from fresh & local ingredients.

Our motto is: "Fresh is the best!"
